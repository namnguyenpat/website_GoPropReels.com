## Red Flags to Watch for in Any Forex Prop Firm

Before buying any evaluation, check for these warning signs:

- **No verifiable payout proof** — always look for trader payout screenshots and community reviews on independent forums
- **Unusual consistency rules** — rules that seem designed to deny payouts rather than manage risk
- **Very high profit targets** — 20%+ in 30 days is unsustainable and designed for failure rates
- **No responsive customer support** — test their chat before buying
- **New firms with no track record** — if they launched in 2025 or 2026 with no established parent company, exercise caution
