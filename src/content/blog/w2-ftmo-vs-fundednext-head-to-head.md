## The Two Giants of Forex Prop Trading

If you are choosing between FTMO and FundedNext — and most serious forex prop traders face this comparison at some point — you are choosing between the two most respected names in the industry. Both have earned their reputations through verifiable payout histories, transparent rule sets, and genuine investment in trader experience.

The comparison is not about which firm is better in absolute terms. It is about which firm is better for your specific trading style, risk tolerance, and operational preferences. Here is the complete picture.

## Evaluation Structure

**FTMO — Two-Phase Classic**

FTMO's Challenge and Verification model is the industry template. Phase 1 requires 10% profit with 10% max drawdown and 5% daily drawdown. Phase 2 requires 5% profit with identical risk parameters. Minimum 4 trading days per phase.

The structure has barely changed since FTMO introduced it — because it works. The consistency rule (no single day can represent more than 30% of total profits) requires traders to distribute gains across multiple sessions rather than relying on single-event luck.

**FundedNext — Multiple Models**

FundedNext offers genuine structural choice:
- **Stellar Two-Step:** 8% Phase 1 / 5% Phase 2 (lower targets than FTMO, no consistency rule)
- **Stellar One-Step:** 10% single phase
- **Express (Instant):** Immediate funding with no evaluation phase

The target difference matters: 8% vs 10% for Phase 1 is a meaningful margin of comfort, particularly for traders operating in volatile instruments like XAUUSD or crypto pairs.

**Edge: FundedNext** on flexibility and lower Phase 1 target. **Edge: FTMO** on evaluation credibility and structure predictability.

## Pricing

Both firms charge evaluation fees based on account size. At the $100,000 account tier (the most popular comparison point):

- **FTMO:** ~$540 for the standard two-phase challenge
- **FundedNext Stellar:** ~$549 for the two-phase challenge

Prices are nearly identical at this tier. FundedNext runs more frequent promotional discounts, which can significantly reduce the comparison price in the trader's favor. FTMO runs periodic promotions but less aggressively than FundedNext.

**Edge: FundedNext** on promotional frequency and discount depth.

## Profit Splits

**FTMO:** 80% base, scaling to 90% after the first payout via the FTMO scaling plan. The scaling plan also increases account size at performance milestones.

**FundedNext:** Stellar accounts start at 90% — no scaling required to reach the top split. Additionally, FundedNext pays 15% of evaluation account profits to traders who pass — a unique feature that effectively reduces your net evaluation cost.

**Edge: FundedNext** — starting at 90% and paying on evaluation profits is genuinely better economics.

## Platform Options

**FTMO:** MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, cTrader, Systeme DXtrade. The broadest platform selection of any major prop firm.

**FundedNext:** DXtrade, MetaTrader 5, TradeLocker. Strong multi-platform offering but MT4 is not available, which matters for traders with MT4-native EAs.

**Edge: FTMO** if you need MT4. **Tie** for traders who work with MT5 or prefer DXtrade/TradeLocker.

## Payout Speed

**FTMO:** 1-5 business days from request to receipt. Reliable and consistent but not the fastest in the industry.

**FundedNext:** Often same-day or next-day processing. [FundedNext](/forex/fundednext-6gkdu) has invested in automated payout infrastructure that makes it among the fastest payers of all major firms.

**Edge: FundedNext** — significantly faster average payout times.

## Community and Support

**FTMO:** Mature, established community. Extensive educational resources. Support is professional and multi-lingual. Response times are good but not instant.

**FundedNext:** Active, growing Discord community. Young but enthusiastic. Support has improved markedly with scale but still occasionally lags FTMO in resolution quality for complex issues.

**Edge: FTMO** on depth of resources and support track record. **Edge: FundedNext** on community energy and responsiveness for straightforward issues.

## The Verdict by Trader Type

**Choose [FTMO](/forex/ftmo-1vldc) if:**
- You use MT4 EAs and need MT4 platform support
- You value institutional credibility and 10+ year track record above all
- Your strategy has a strong win rate and the consistency rule is manageable
- You prefer conservative evaluation structures with fewer variables

**Choose [FundedNext](/forex/fundednext-6gkdu) if:**
- You want to start at 90% profit split immediately
- You value faster payouts as a priority
- You prefer lower Phase 1 profit targets (8% vs 10%)
- You trade through DXtrade or MT5 and do not need MT4

For most traders in 2026, running accounts at both firms is the optimal answer. The cost of running an evaluation at each provides genuine comparison data from your own trading, and having funded accounts at both gives you firm diversification that benefits you regardless of which comparison wins academically.
