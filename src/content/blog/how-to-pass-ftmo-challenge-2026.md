## Key Strategy Tips

### 1. Risk 1% Per Trade Maximum
FTMO's own guidelines recommend 1–1.5% risk per trade. Staying at 1% gives you 10 losing trades before hitting the daily limit — plenty of room.

### 2. Avoid News Trading (Standard Funded)
In the Standard Funded stage, news trading is prohibited 2 minutes before and after high-impact events. Use Swing accounts if you need news freedom.

### 3. Use the Consistency Rule Wisely (1-Step)
The 1-Step Challenge has a Best Day Rule: no single day can account for more than 50% of total profit. Spread your gains evenly.
