# Fintokei: Globally Accessible Futures and Forex Funding

Fintokei was built with international traders in mind, offering evaluation programs that support multi-currency payments and cater to traders from regions often underserved by Western-focused prop firms. Headquartered in Prague, Fintokei has developed a solid reputation for transparency, responsive customer support, and fair evaluation conditions across both forex and futures markets.

## Key Highlights

- **Global-First Design**: Multi-currency fee payments and support for traders across Asia, Eastern Europe, Latin America, and beyond.
- **Dual Market Access**: Programs available for both forex/CFD and CME futures traders.
- **Transparent Operations**: Clear, published rules with a strong community presence and regular communication from the firm.

## Who It's For

Fintokei is the top choice for international traders who feel overlooked by US-centric or EU-focused prop firms. It also suits traders who want a firm with a strong community, regular updates, and genuine attention to the global trader audience.
