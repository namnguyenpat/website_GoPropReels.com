# Maven: A Different Way to Think About Funded Futures

Maven Trading stands apart from conventional futures prop firms through its subscription-based model — rather than paying a one-time evaluation fee and hoping to pass, traders pay a recurring monthly fee for access to a funded account with a profit-sharing arrangement. This approach aligns incentives between the firm and the trader, reducing pressure and encouraging long-term, sustainable trading behavior over rapid challenge-passing tactics.

## Key Highlights

- **Subscription Model**: Monthly fee access to funded futures accounts without the pass/fail pressure of traditional evaluations.
- **No Evaluation Phase**: Traders begin trading with a live funded account from day one of their subscription.
- **CME Futures Access**: Trade popular futures contracts including ES, NQ, CL, and more.

## Who It's For

Maven is perfect for consistent futures traders who find traditional evaluation models psychologically challenging or who've had bad luck with sudden rule violations. The subscription model suits traders who want to trade sustainably over months rather than rushing through a challenge window.
