# Alpha Capital: Straightforward Forex Funding

Alpha Capital Group entered the prop trading scene with a focus on accessibility and clean program structure. Offering forex and CFD trading on MetaTrader 5, the firm provides 1-step and 2-step evaluation paths with clearly defined rules. Their profit split starts at 80% and can scale up, making them an attractive option for traders looking for a no-frills funded account.

## Key Highlights

- **Dual Evaluation Options**: Choose between a 1-step or 2-step challenge to match your preferred risk style.
- **Up to 90% Profit Split**: Scaling plans reward consistency with higher payout percentages over time.
- **No Minimum Trading Days**: Pass the evaluation at your own pace without forced time requirements.

## Who It's For

Alpha Capital suits forex traders who prefer flexibility in their evaluation path and want a clean, transparent rule set without overly complex conditions. It's a good fit for intermediate traders building toward a consistent track record.
