# FundedNext Futures: A Powerhouse Brand Enters Futures

FundedNext became one of the fastest-growing forex prop firms in history, and their expansion into futures brings that same momentum to the CME space. FundedNext Futures offers traders access to popular index and commodity futures with evaluation rules that are competitive and designed to be genuinely passable by skilled traders. The firm's massive community, frequent promotions, and strong social presence translate directly into the futures program.

## Key Highlights

- **Market-Leading Community**: Tap into one of the largest prop trading communities for support, strategy sharing, and motivation.
- **Frequent Promotional Discounts**: FundedNext regularly offers deep discounts on evaluation fees, making entry cost-effective.
- **Strong Payout Infrastructure**: Built on FundedNext's proven financial operations with reliable withdrawal processing.

## Who It's For

FundedNext Futures is ideal for traders already in the FundedNext ecosystem who want to diversify into futures, as well as futures traders seeking the support of a large, active community. It's particularly well-suited to traders who appreciate a firm that invests heavily in its brand and community.
