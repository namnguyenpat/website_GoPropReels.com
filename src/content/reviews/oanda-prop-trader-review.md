# OANDA Prop Trader: Where Legacy Brokerage Meets Modern Funding

OANDA is one of the most recognized names in retail forex, with global regulatory licenses and decades of operational history. OANDA Prop Trader brings that credibility into the funded trading space, offering evaluation-based programs that trade on OANDA's own platform infrastructure. For traders who've always wanted to combine the trust of a regulated broker with the capital leverage of a prop firm, this is a compelling proposition.

## Key Highlights

- **Regulated Broker Backing**: Trade through a firm regulated by the FCA, CFTC, IIROC, and others — not an offshore entity.
- **OANDA Platform Access**: Use the familiar, award-winning OANDA fxTrade platform rather than a third-party MT setup.
- **Established Financial Infrastructure**: Benefit from OANDA's robust technology and financial operations.

## Who It's For

OANDA Prop Trader is ideal for traders who prioritize regulatory credibility and platform familiarity above aggressive promotional pricing. It's an excellent fit for traders from regulated markets who want the added security of prop funding through a broker they already know and trust.
