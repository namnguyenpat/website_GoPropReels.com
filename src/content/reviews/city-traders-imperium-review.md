# City Traders Imperium: More Than Just a Prop Firm

City Traders Imperium (CTI) is one of the most respected names in the forex prop trading industry. Founded in 2015 in the UK, CTI operates with a genuine trader-development mindset — offering evaluation programs alongside mentorship, a trading community, and educational resources that set it apart from purely evaluation-focused firms. Their non-MTI evaluation path and the Direct Funding option are particularly well-regarded in the industry.

## Key Highlights

- **Direct Funding Available**: Skip the challenge phase entirely and receive immediate access to a funded account.
- **Scaling Up to $4M**: An industry-leading scaling plan that rewards profitable traders with substantial capital growth.
- **Trader Development Focus**: Access to mentorship, group sessions, and educational materials alongside the funded program.

## Who It's For

CTI is the ideal firm for serious forex traders who want more than just capital — they want to grow as professionals. It particularly suits traders with a longer time horizon who value mentorship, accountability, and a structured path to larger funding levels.
