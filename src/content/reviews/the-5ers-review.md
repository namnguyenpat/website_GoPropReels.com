# The 5%ers: Built for Traders Playing the Long Game

The 5%ers stands as one of the most unique and ambitious prop firms in the forex industry. Founded in 2016, they have consistently focused on developing professional-grade traders through a scaling program that begins modestly and grows to accounts as large as $4,000,000 in funded capital. Their philosophy centers on building real traders, not just selling evaluations — a distinction clearly visible in their rule design and community culture.

## Key Highlights

- **Scaling to $4,000,000**: One of the most aggressive and credible scaling programs in the entire forex prop industry.
- **Proprietary Platform Option**: The 5%ers uses their own trading platform alongside MT5, offering unique tools and analytics.
- **Bootcamp Program**: An entry-level program specifically designed to develop traders from the ground up before granting funded status.

## Who It's For

The 5%ers is the ideal firm for patient, career-oriented forex traders who are thinking in years, not weeks. It's particularly suited to swing traders and position traders with solid risk management who want to build toward genuinely large capital allocations over time.
