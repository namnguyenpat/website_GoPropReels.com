# Trade The Pool: Prop Funding Beyond Forex and Futures

Trade The Pool fills a critical gap in the prop trading industry — funded accounts for stock and equity traders. While most prop firms focus on forex or futures, Trade The Pool caters to traders who specialize in US equities, ETFs, and individual stocks. This unique positioning makes it the go-to firm for day traders and swing traders working in the world's largest equity markets with a familiar stock scanner and L2 toolset.

- **Stocks and ETF Focus**: One of the only prop firms offering funded accounts specifically for US equity markets.
- **Large Account Sizes**: Funded allocations scaling from tens of thousands to over $1,000,000 in buying power.
- **Stock-Specific Evaluation**: Challenge rules calibrated for equities trading dynamics rather than forex or futures volatility.

## Who It's For

Trade The Pool is the obvious choice for equity day traders and swing traders who've been underserved by a prop industry dominated by forex and futures firms. If your edge is in reading Level 2 data, trading earnings, or working with US equities, Trade The Pool is built specifically for your style.
