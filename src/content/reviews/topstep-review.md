# Topstep: The Firm That Started It All

Topstep is the original funded futures prop firm, having pioneered the evaluation-based model for CME traders when it launched in 2012. Over a decade later, Topstep remains the benchmark against which all other funded futures programs are measured. With hundreds of millions paid out to traders, a deeply established community, and continuous product innovation, Topstep's leadership position in the futures funding space is well-earned and still growing.

## Key Highlights

- **Originator of the Model**: The firm that created funded futures trading as we know it — with over a decade of proven payout history.
- **Comprehensive Trader Support**: Access to a dedicated coaching staff, performance analytics, and a massive community of funded traders.
- **Combined Accounts**: Funded traders can combine multiple accounts up to $150,000 for increased buying power.

## Who It's For

Topstep is the right choice for any serious futures trader — from beginners entering the funded space for the first time, to experienced traders who want the prestige and support of the industry's founding firm. The combination of history, payouts, and community is unmatched.
