## FTMO Overview

FTMO, founded in 2015 in the Czech Republic, is one of the most established and trusted prop trading firms globally. With over 3.5 million customers and $500M+ in payouts, FTMO sets the industry standard for evaluation-based prop trading.

## Trading Programs

FTMO offers two primary evaluation paths: the **Standard 2-Step Challenge** and the newer **1-Step Challenge** (launched February 2026). Account sizes range from $10,000 to $200,000.

## Key Rules

- **Profit Target**: 10% (Phase 1), 5% (Phase 2)
- **Max Daily Loss**: 5%
- **Max Overall Loss**: 10%
- **Profit Split**: Up to 90%
- **News Trading**: Allowed in Evaluation, restricted in Funded (Standard)

## Why FTMO?

FTMO's acquisition of OANDA in 2025 signals a move toward fully regulated infrastructure, making it one of the safest choices for serious traders.
