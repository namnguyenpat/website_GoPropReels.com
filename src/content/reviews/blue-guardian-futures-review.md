# Blue Guardian Futures: Solid Ground in the Futures Space

Blue Guardian Futures is the futures-focused arm of the established Blue Guardian prop firm, bringing their reputation for reliability and trader support into CME-listed markets. The program covers popular futures contracts including equity indices and commodities, with evaluation rules that are clear and consistently enforced.

## Key Highlights

- **Established Brand Trust**: Benefits from Blue Guardian's track record of consistent payouts and transparent operations.
- **CME-Listed Instruments**: Trade ES, NQ, GC, CL, and other liquid futures markets.
- **Trader-Friendly Drawdown Rules**: Designed to give traders room to manage positions without triggering unnecessary violations.

## Who It's For

Blue Guardian Futures is well-suited for futures traders who want the backing of a known, reliable brand rather than taking a chance on an untested firm. It's a good choice for traders transitioning from forex prop firms into the futures space and seeking a trusted name to start with.
