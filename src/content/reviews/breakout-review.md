# Breakout: Built for Traders Who Move Fast

Breakout entered the forex prop space with a focus on speed and simplicity — from onboarding to getting funded. Offering both instant funding and traditional challenge-based evaluation, Breakout caters to traders across the experience spectrum. The firm operates on MetaTrader platforms and supports a wide range of forex pairs and CFDs with competitive spreads and execution.

## Key Highlights

- **Instant Funding Option**: Skip the challenge entirely and go straight to a funded account for a one-time fee.
- **Traditional Challenge Route**: Standard 2-phase evaluation for traders who prefer proving their edge first.
- **High Profit Splits**: Payout percentages go up to 90% for top-performing funded traders.

## Who It's For

Breakout is best for forex traders who are confident in their strategy and want to either skip the evaluation process or move through it quickly. The instant funding option makes it especially attractive for experienced traders who prefer paying a premium for immediate access to capital.
