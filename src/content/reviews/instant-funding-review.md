# Instant Funding: Capital on Demand for Confident Traders

Instant Funding does exactly what its name promises — it eliminates the evaluation step entirely and gives traders immediate access to a funded forex account. For traders who are confident in their strategy and simply want to begin trading with capital as soon as possible, the instant model removes the primary pain point of prop trading. The firm supports MetaTrader platforms with a range of forex and CFD instruments.

## Key Highlights

- **No Evaluation Required**: Pay a one-time fee and begin trading a funded account immediately — no challenge, no waiting.
- **Full Profit Retention**: High profit split arrangements that make the upfront cost worthwhile for consistent performers.
- **Scalable Accounts**: Options to grow your funded allocation as your track record develops.

## Who It's For

Instant Funding is purpose-built for experienced forex traders who are confident enough in their edge to skip the evaluation process. It's particularly valuable for traders who have previously failed evaluations due to rule violations rather than unprofitability, or those who simply want to begin generating returns from day one.
