# ATFunded: Getting Traders Funded Without the Hassle

ATFunded positions itself as an accessible entry point into forex prop trading, offering evaluation programs designed to minimize unnecessary complexity. With a focus on forex and major CFD pairs, ATFunded provides traders a straightforward challenge structure with competitive profit splits starting at 80% and scaling over time.

## Key Highlights

- **Simple Evaluation Structure**: Clean, easy-to-understand profit targets and drawdown rules without hidden clauses.
- **Competitive Profit Split**: Start at 80% and grow your share through consistent performance.
- **Multiple Account Sizes**: Options ranging from small starter accounts up to larger funded allocations to suit different risk profiles.

## Who It's For

ATFunded is a solid starting point for forex traders who are new to prop firm evaluations or want a straightforward, affordable program. It also appeals to experienced traders seeking a secondary funded account with minimal overhead costs.
