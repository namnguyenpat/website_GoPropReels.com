# Axi Select: Where Broker Regulation Meets Prop Funding

Axi Select stands out in the prop trading world because it's backed by Axi, a globally regulated forex and CFD broker with licenses in multiple jurisdictions. Unlike most prop firms, Axi Select uses a performance-based model where traders start with a demo account, prove their skills, and progress through funding stages — with no upfront challenge fee. This talent-identification approach makes it one of the most unique offerings in the space.

## Key Highlights

- **No Upfront Fee**: Traders begin with a free evaluation, making it zero-risk to enter the program.
- **Broker-Grade Infrastructure**: Trade on Axi's regulated MT4/MT5 infrastructure with professional execution.
- **Performance-Based Scaling**: Capital increases are tied directly to demonstrated trading performance and risk management.

## Who It's For

Axi Select is perfect for confident forex traders who believe in their edge and don't want to pay evaluation fees upfront. The program also suits traders who prefer the security of trading within a regulated broker environment rather than a standalone prop firm.
