# TradeDay: Freedom to Trade on Your Own Terms

TradeDay has earned a strong following in the funded futures community primarily because of one powerful feature: no consistency rule. Where many firms restrict how much of your profit can come from a single trading day, TradeDay removes this constraint entirely. This trader-friendly stance, combined with competitive pricing and solid CME instrument access, has made TradeDay one of the most popular mid-tier futures prop firms.

## Key Highlights

- **No Consistency Rule**: Trade freely without worrying about a single big day disqualifying your evaluation — a major advantage.
- **Competitive Evaluation Fees**: Affordable entry pricing with frequent promotional discounts available.
- **CME Instrument Access**: Trade popular equity index, metals, and energy futures with professional-grade execution.

## Who It's For

TradeDay is perfect for futures traders with high-conviction trading styles who may land big gains on fewer, larger days. It's especially appealing to breakout traders, news traders, and others whose natural trading patterns are penalized by consistency rules at other firms.
