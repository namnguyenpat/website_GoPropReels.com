# Take Profit Trader: The Futures Firm That Actually Wants You to Pass

Take Profit Trader (TPT) has built a strong reputation by taking an unusual stance in the prop world — they design their rules to genuinely help traders pass, not to create traps. With some of the most lenient and clearly structured evaluation parameters in the funded futures space, TPT has attracted a devoted following of traders who feel underserved by stricter firms. Their community presence and transparent communication reinforce this trader-first identity.

## Key Highlights

- **Trader-Friendly Evaluation Rules**: Designed with real traders in mind — clear profit targets and reasonable drawdown limits.
- **No Consistency Rule**: Unlike many futures firms, TPT does not impose restrictive consistency requirements on trading days.
- **Active Community Presence**: Engage with a growing community of funded traders across social channels and live Q&A sessions.

## Who It's For

Take Profit Trader is a standout choice for futures traders who've felt frustrated by overly complex or punitive evaluation rules at other firms. It's ideal for traders who trade with conviction on fewer high-quality setups and don't want to be penalized for asymmetric daily performance.
