# Apex Trader Funding: Futures Trading at Scale

Apex Trader Funding has grown into the most popular funded futures firm in the industry, and for good reason. Founded in 2021, Apex offers some of the largest account sizes available — up to $300,000 — with rules that are consistently cited as among the most trader-friendly on the market. Their rapid rise to the top was fueled by transparent payouts, frequent promotional pricing, and a loyal community of futures traders.

## Key Highlights

- **Massive Account Sizes**: Choose from accounts ranging from $25,000 up to $300,000 in simulated buying power.
- **Generous Consistency Rule**: Only 30% of your best trading day counts toward the consistency cap, keeping rules relaxed.
- **Frequent Promotions**: Apex regularly offers 80–90% off evaluation fees, making entry extremely affordable.

## Who It's For

Apex is the go-to choice for futures traders who want scale, straightforward rules, and a proven payout track record. It suits both full-time traders and those building their track record in CME-listed instruments like ES, NQ, CL, and Gold.
