# ThinkCapital: Broker-Backed Prop Funding with Genuine Credentials

ThinkCapital is the prop trading division of ThinkMarkets, a globally regulated forex and CFD broker with licenses across multiple jurisdictions including the FCA, ASIC, and FSCA. This broker backing gives ThinkCapital a level of credibility that few standalone prop firms can match. The program offers evaluation-based forex funding with access to ThinkMarkets' professional MT4/MT5 infrastructure and execution quality.

## Key Highlights

- **Globally Regulated Backer**: ThinkMarkets holds licenses across FCA, ASIC, FSCA, and more — providing real regulatory oversight.
- **Professional Execution**: Trade on the same infrastructure used by ThinkMarkets' institutional and retail clients.
- **Transparent Program Structure**: Clear evaluation rules backed by an organization with years of verifiable financial operations.

## Who It's For

ThinkCapital is the right fit for forex traders who want the security of trading within a regulated broker ecosystem rather than risking capital with an unregulated standalone prop firm. It particularly appeals to traders in regulated markets like Australia, the UK, and South Africa who value compliance.
