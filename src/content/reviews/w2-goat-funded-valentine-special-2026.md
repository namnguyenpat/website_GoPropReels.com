## Love, Trading, and Record Numbers

Valentine's Day is not a holiday traditionally associated with financial markets. But in the prop trading world — where promotional campaigns are a primary driver of evaluation account sales — February 14 has become a significant commercial event. In 2026, GOAT Funded turned it into a milestone moment.

The firm's Valentine's Day promotional campaign, which ran from February 12-14, 2026, delivered evaluation account discounts of up to 75%, bundled with bonus trading capital for accounts purchased during the promotional window. The result: over 3,000 evaluation accounts purchased in 24 hours at the campaign's peak — the highest single-day sales volume in GOAT Funded's operational history.

## The Campaign Structure

GOAT Funded's Valentine's special was designed with a tiered structure that rewarded both new and returning traders:

**New trader offer:** 75% off evaluation fees on the first evaluation purchased, across all account sizes from $10,000 to $200,000.

**Returning trader offer:** 60% off any additional evaluation, plus a 5% bonus to the funded account starting balance for traders who had previously passed an evaluation with GOAT.

**Referral multiplier:** During the Valentine's window, existing traders who referred new buyers earned double the standard referral bonus — pushing some referral payouts to $200 per new trader.

**Time pressure:** The full 75% discount was only available for 24 hours before stepping down to 50% for the remaining promotional window. This urgency mechanic drove concentrated purchase activity and contributed to the single-day record.

## Why Promotional Events Drive This Kind of Response

The prop trading community is highly responsive to promotional pricing because evaluation accounts are fundamentally discretionary purchases. A trader who intends to eventually purchase an evaluation is willing to accelerate that decision when the price is significantly reduced.

At 75% off, an evaluation for a $50,000 account that normally costs $300 is available for $75. For a trader who has been preparing and intends to attempt the challenge eventually, waiting for a promotional event is pure financial rationality. Firms that understand this dynamic structure their promotional calendars deliberately to pull forward purchase intent that already exists.

The Valentine's timing is not arbitrary either. February follows the post-Christmas spending recovery period and precedes the spring trading season — a period when trader enthusiasm and market conditions both tend to improve.

## GOAT Funded's Growth Trajectory

The Valentine's milestone fits within GOAT Funded's broader growth narrative. The firm has positioned itself in the competitive mid-market of forex prop trading — not targeting the budget segment nor competing directly on premium brand with [FTMO](/forex/ftmo-1vldc), but offering competitive terms with aggressive promotional pricing for traders who know how to shop the calendar.

The firm's Discord community has grown significantly in the months surrounding these promotional events, as traders who purchased during a discount become community members who discuss and share their experiences — creating organic retention and referral activity.
