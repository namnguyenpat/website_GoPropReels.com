# Ment Funding: Funding for the Long Haul

Ment Funding differentiates itself in the crowded forex prop space with its standout lifetime reset policy — if a funded trader violates their account, they can reset without paying full price again. This model demonstrates genuine commitment to trader longevity over a churn-and-burn approach. Operating across MetaTrader platforms with forex and major CFD pairs, Ment Funding has built a loyal following of traders who appreciate being treated as long-term partners.

## Key Highlights

- **Lifetime Reset Policy**: Funded account violations can be reset at a reduced cost, giving traders multiple chances to succeed.
- **Flexible Evaluation Options**: Choose from multiple challenge structures to find the best fit for your trading style.
- **Growing Payout Record**: A steadily expanding community with verified payouts building their reputation.

## Who It's For

Ment Funding is ideal for forex traders who want a firm that invests in their success over the long term. It's particularly well-suited to traders who've experienced the frustration of losing a funded account to a single bad day and want a firm that gives them a genuine second chance.
