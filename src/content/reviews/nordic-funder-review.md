# Nordic Funder: Precision-Built Forex Funding from the North

Nordic Funder brings a distinctly European sensibility to forex prop trading — methodical, transparent, and built for longevity. Based in Scandinavia, the firm has carved out a strong reputation for operational excellence and a genuine commitment to treating traders fairly. Their challenge programs on MetaTrader platforms feature competitive profit splits and clear rules without the gimmicks that have plagued other firms.

## Key Highlights

- **European Operational Standards**: High standards for compliance, transparency, and trader communication baked into the firm's DNA.
- **Competitive Profit Splits**: Up to 90% payout for traders who demonstrate consistent, disciplined performance.
- **No Surprise Rules**: Clean, straightforward evaluation conditions with no hidden clauses designed to disqualify traders.

## Who It's For

Nordic Funder is particularly appealing to European forex traders who want to work with a firm that shares their expectations for transparency and professionalism. It's also a great fit for traders globally who prioritize operational reliability and clear communication over flashy marketing.
