## The Credibility Marker That Matters

In the prop trading industry, payout milestones function as the closest available equivalent to an external audit. When a firm announces it has paid a specific, verifiable amount to traders, it is making a claim that can be partially validated through community evidence — the traders who received payouts, the payout screenshots shared publicly, the pattern of verified reviews on independent platforms.

Funded Trading Plus — the UK-based prop firm that has built a loyal following through transparent operations and a clear challenge structure — crossed its $10 million total payout milestone in early 2026. For a firm of its size and age, this is a meaningful demonstration of operational sustainability.

## Funded Trading Plus: Background

Funded Trading Plus launched with a proposition that positioned it as a serious alternative to larger, better-known competitors: straightforward rules, competitive profit splits, and an emphasis on trader education as part of the evaluation process. The firm's UK base gave it a different regulatory and cultural orientation than the predominantly Dubai-based or Eastern European prop firm operators.

Its evaluation structure features:
- One-step and two-step challenge options
- Account sizes from $5,000 to $200,000
- Profit targets of 8-10% depending on account type
- Daily drawdown limits of 5%, max drawdown of 10%
- Profit split beginning at 80% and scaling toward 90%

The $10 million milestone announcement cited verified payout data across the firm's trader population, with the breakdown showing approximately 60% of payouts coming from funded accounts in the £25,000-£100,000 range — consistent with a trader demographic that is serious but not at the highest capital tier.

## What Community Data Shows

Independent verification of Funded Trading Plus's payout claims draws on several sources:

- Trustpilot reviews from funded traders citing specific payout amounts and timelines
- Discord and Reddit community threads featuring payout confirmation screenshots
- Comparison with peer firms at similar scale and age, where the payout trajectory is consistent

The picture that emerges: Funded Trading Plus processes payouts reliably, with typical turnaround times of 1-3 business days from request to receipt. The firm does not match the headline payout speed of the fastest operators in the industry, but has not generated the complaints about delayed or denied payouts that have damaged other firms' reputations.

## The UK Firm Ecosystem

Funded Trading Plus operates in a corner of the prop industry that deserves more attention: the UK-based or UK-oriented firm ecosystem. Several other firms with UK roots or strong UK trader communities have carved out loyal followings separate from the global mainstream.

UK traders in particular benefit from firms that understand the local banking infrastructure, tax reporting context, and regulatory environment. Funded Trading Plus has positioned itself as a UK-first firm even as it accepts international traders.

## What the $10M Milestone Means for Prospective Traders

For a trader evaluating Funded Trading Plus as an option, the $10 million payout milestone provides useful context but is not sufficient due diligence on its own. Key additional considerations:

- The milestone is cumulative, not monthly — Funded Trading Plus is not operating at the scale of FTMO or [FundedNext](/forex/fundednext-6gkdu), and traders should calibrate expectations accordingly
- Community reviews remain the most reliable signal of current operational quality
- The firm's two-step challenge option is a competitive differentiator for traders who prefer lower profit targets and more trading time

For traders who want an established, reliable UK-oriented prop firm as part of their portfolio, Funded Trading Plus's track record and $10 million milestone make it a credible option alongside the global giants.
