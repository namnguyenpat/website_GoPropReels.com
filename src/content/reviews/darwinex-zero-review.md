# Darwinex Zero: Prop Trading Backed by Real Regulatory Credibility

Darwinex Zero is not your average prop firm — it's the funded trader program of Darwinex, a fully regulated investment firm authorized by the FCA in the UK and CNMV in Spain. What makes Darwinex Zero exceptional is the pathway it offers: traders who prove their skills can have their strategy turned into a DARWIN — a tradeable asset that institutional investors can allocate capital to. This creates an income stream beyond simple profit splits.

## Key Highlights

- **FCA & CNMV Regulated**: Operating under genuine regulatory oversight, not as an unregulated offshore entity.
- **DARWIN Asset Creation**: Successful traders can generate income from external investor allocations, not just their own trading.
- **Subscription-Based Model**: Pay a monthly fee rather than a one-time challenge fee for ongoing access.

## Who It's For

Darwinex Zero is the ideal firm for highly skilled forex traders who want to build a long-term career managing institutional capital. It's a natural fit for systematic and algorithmic traders with robust strategies that can attract external investment through the DARWIN marketplace.
