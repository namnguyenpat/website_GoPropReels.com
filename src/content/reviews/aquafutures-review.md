# AquaFutures: A Rising Name in Funded Futures

AquaFutures is a newer futures-focused prop firm designed for traders who value clarity and speed. Built around CME-listed instruments, AquaFutures emphasizes transparency in its evaluation rules and a quick turnaround from passing to first payout. The firm has been gaining traction in the funded futures community for its approachable fee structure and responsive support.

## Key Highlights

- **Clear Rule Structure**: Straightforward drawdown and profit targets with no hidden conditions that trip up traders.
- **Fast Payout Processing**: AquaFutures prioritizes quick withdrawal processing once traders reach funded status.
- **Affordable Entry**: Low evaluation fees make it easy to enter multiple accounts and build momentum.

## Who It's For

AquaFutures is ideal for futures traders who are tired of opaque rule sets and slow payout processes. It suits traders at any level who want a clean, no-surprise environment to prove their edge in the CME markets.
