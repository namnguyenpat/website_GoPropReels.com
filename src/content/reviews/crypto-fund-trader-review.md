# Crypto Fund Trader: Funding the Digital Asset Revolution

Crypto Fund Trader fills a unique niche in the prop trading space — it's one of the few firms dedicated entirely to cryptocurrency trading. Rather than treating crypto as an afterthought alongside forex or futures, Crypto Fund Trader has built its entire infrastructure around digital assets. Traders can evaluate and get funded to trade Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major crypto pairs with evaluations designed around crypto's natural volatility.

## Key Highlights

- **Crypto-Only Focus**: Evaluations and funded accounts built specifically for cryptocurrency market dynamics.
- **24/7 Market Access**: Trade crypto around the clock, including weekends, unlike forex or futures programs.
- **Volatility-Adjusted Rules**: Drawdown and profit targets calibrated for crypto's larger price swings rather than forex-style parameters.

## Who It's For

Crypto Fund Trader is the obvious choice for traders whose expertise lies in digital assets and who find forex or futures prop firm rules ill-suited to crypto trading. It's ideal for technical traders who thrive in 24/7 markets and want to capitalize on crypto's unique volatility profile.
