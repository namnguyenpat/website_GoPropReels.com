# E8 Futures: Quality Futures Funding from a Proven Name

E8 Futures is the futures division of E8 Markets, a well-regarded prop firm known for its professionalism and strong trader community. Bringing that same standard into CME-listed markets, E8 Futures offers traders access to equity index, energy, and metals futures with evaluation programs that mirror the quality standards the E8 brand is known for. Their payout track record and operational reliability carry over seamlessly from the forex side.

## Key Highlights

- **Established Brand Backing**: Benefits from E8 Markets' strong reputation for fair play and consistent operations.
- **CME Instrument Coverage**: Trade popular futures markets including ES, NQ, CL, and GC.
- **Competitive Drawdown Parameters**: Evaluation rules designed to give traders a genuine opportunity rather than trapping them in technicalities.

## Who It's For

E8 Futures is ideal for traders already familiar with the E8 brand who want to expand into futures, as well as futures specialists seeking a firm with proven reliability. It's particularly suited to traders who value a firm's operational history over cutting-edge promotional offers.
