# Alpha Futures: Entering the Futures Funding Arena

Alpha Futures is a newer entrant in the funded futures space, aiming to differentiate through competitive evaluation pricing and a simplified rule structure. The firm focuses on CME-traded instruments including equity index futures and energy contracts, providing traders access to real market depth through reputable execution platforms.

## Key Highlights

- **Competitive Evaluation Fees**: Entry costs are kept low to make futures funding accessible to a wider audience.
- **Simplified Rules**: Fewer restrictions around trading hours and news events compared to more conservative competitors.
- **Fast-Track to Funding**: Streamlined evaluation stages designed to get traders funded quickly.

## Who It's For

Alpha Futures is well-suited for newer futures traders who want affordable access to the funded space without the complexity of strict rule sets. It's also appealing to experienced traders looking for a low-cost secondary account to run alongside a main funded program.
